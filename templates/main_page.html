A Bit About Me

During my last semester as an Information Technology & Systems major, I was matched with Siliconvalley4U for my senior capstone project to build a web portal tool for the company. Despite having limited coding experience initially, I quickly became our team lead and learned on the fly how to work with JSON, Firebase, and Java syntax. This experience sparked my interest in programming, leading me to switch careers and join the company as an apprentice after graduating.

Since then, I've had the opportunity to contribute to some unique startup projects and have grown a full-stack skill set. I now teach coding classes to kids at Siliconvalley4U while working part-time at Nexclap, an e-learning platform startup.

This journey has helped me discover my passion, expand my technical skills, and fuel my continuous drive to learn. Looking ahead, I'm excited to continue my career growth and work on some cool projects!
