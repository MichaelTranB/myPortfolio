Valentine's Card Website

A static website I made complete with music, a personalized and interactive polaroid gallery with a locked note, and plenty of confetti.

For the password protected note I used a combination of Netlify's serverless functions and the client side JavaScript to verify the PIN and then securely retrieve and serve the actual note's image in a base64-encoded format directly to the browser.
