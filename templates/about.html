A Bit About Me ...

As an Information Technology & Systems major, my path took an unexpected turn during my senior year capstone project with Siliconvalley4U. Initially only having limited coding knowledge, I found myself heavily involved in building a web portal tool for the company, which not only transformed the trajectory of my career but also sparked a deep interest in software development.

This newfound passion led me to work with innovative startups such as Yatsys, a company focusing on IoT-based home care for children with disablities, and Nexclap, a community oriented e-learning platform. There, I honed my skills in Python, Javascript, Flask, Tensorflow, and more, solidifying my foundation and shaping me into a dedicated software engineer.

In this journey, I've learned that software engineering extends beyond mere lines of code—it's about fostering a collaborative spirit. I excel in team dynamics, merging my technical know-how with strong people skills—an attribute refined through teaching coding to younger students and creating engaging educational content.

Fueled by my continuous drive to learn, I'm constantly expanding my skill set and love tackling complex problems. With a diverse experience portfolio, a love for innovation, and a strong commitment to growth, I am ready to join a team of forward-thinkers and together, create technology that makes an impact. Together, let's shape the future!
